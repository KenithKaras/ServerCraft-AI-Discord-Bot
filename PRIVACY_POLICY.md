# Privacy Policy for ServerCraft AI

*Last Updated: [Date]*

Thank you for using **ServerCraft AI**! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Discord bot. We believe in keeping things simple and respect your privacy.

## 1. What Data We Collect & How We Use It

When you use the `/setup` command, ServerCraft AI processes the text instructions you provide. 
- **Temporary Processing**: We only use your text instructions temporarily to generate the requested server structure (roles, categories, and channels).
- **Third-Party AI Providers**: To understand your instructions and build a structure, your text prompt is sent to third-party AI service providers (specifically **Groq** and **OpenRouter**). Please do not include sensitive or personal information in your setup prompts.
- **No Permanent Storage**: We **do not** permanently store your text prompts, personal data, chat messages, server member lists, or any other content from your Discord server.

## 2. Server Permissions

To function correctly, ServerCraft AI requires the `Administrator` or `Manage Roles` & `Manage Channels` permissions. 
- We only use these permissions to create the roles, categories, and channels you explicitly requested. 
- The bot will **never** read your chat history or modify existing server structures without your direct command and explicit confirmation.

## 3. Third-Party Services

Since we rely on external AI APIs (Groq and OpenRouter) to process your text prompts, your prompts are subject to their respective privacy policies. They process the data to return the structured JSON required to build your server.

## 4. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally as the bot evolves. Significant changes will be announced in our support server.
